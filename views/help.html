Summarization Tool Overview

The primary function of our Summarization Tool is to make lengthy, difficult-to-read privacy disclosures more accessible. You can enter helpful information that you would like the Generative AI system to consider, and the output will use the input information to try to be more helpful. The tool asks for some inputs that are outlined below.

Age

As outlined by our Terms of Use, you must be 18 years of age or older to use this tool. However, you are free to enter any age you wish - the age you pass to the tool will pass to the Generative AI backend to help it form a more relevant summary for your input age.

Level of Education

This dropdown menu allows you to select a relevant Level of Education. The options provided are "Graduate", "College", "High School", "Middle School", "Elementary School", and "Undetermined". For the most personally relevant output, we encourage selecting the highest level of education completed, if possible; as with the Age input, you do not need to provide your actual level of education.

Privacy Understanding (1-10)

The Privacy Understanding scale allows you to indicate your familiarity with privacy policies and data protection concepts on a scale of 1 to 10. A rating of 1 indicates very limited knowledge of privacy concepts, while 10 indicates expert-level understanding. This helps the AI tailor explanations to your comfort level — for example, someone rating themselves as a 2 may receive explanations that replace technical terms with conceptual explanations, while someone rating themselves as a 9 might receive a response that makes liberal use of technical terminology.

Some examples of terms you may see in a privacy policy that may be unfamiliar include:

  • VPN
  • IP Address
  • PII
  • Data Controller
  • Cookies
  • Third Party
  • Analytics
  • GDPR
  • CCPA
  • Data Subject
  • Data Processing
  • Opt-Out
  • SSL/TLS
  • API
  • Data Breach
  • Data Retention
  • Encryption
  • Data Mining

There are additional terms not listed here, but you can use this sample list of terms to understand what level of Privacy Understanding may be most relevant to you.

Processing Speed

You can choose between "Fast", "Medium", and "Slow" processing speeds.

Privacy Policy Content

This is the input area where you paste the privacy policy you wish to summarize.

Comparison Tool Overview

The Comparison Tool allows you to compare the contents of two privacy policies.

The Comparison Tool is designed to allow you to compare the contents of two privacy policies. There are a number of services available on the Web that have varying degrees of privacy awareness — an example is DuckDuckGo, which aims to be a privacy-conscious alternative to Google Search with clear claims upfront about minimal user tracking. Other services are less explicit about their privacy impact versus that of their competitors; an example would be Uber versus Lyft, two ridesharing services where neither service markets itself as being more privacy conscious than the other. The Comparison Tool aims to be helpful in either scenario.

The Comparison Tool is informed by an identical set of parameters that you are able to supply via the Comparison Tool's page. The one additional input compared to the Summary Tool is another text box for a privacy policy input that allows you to provide two policies for comparison instead of just one like the Summarization Tool.
